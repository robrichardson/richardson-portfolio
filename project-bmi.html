THE BRIEF

Brand identity and logo design for BMI On The Spot, a series of health and wellbeing workshops run by the BMI Alexandra hospital in Manchester. The brief was to create a bold, approachable identity that would stand out in a healthcare context and communicate accessibility and community, positioned as friendly and open rather than clinical.
