CENTAURI'S
SHADOW

Client Ross Garner
Year 2024
Services Cover Design, Book Layout

THE BRIEF

Ross Garner approached me to design the cover for his debut sci-fi novel, and I eagerly accepted the opportunity. After receiving the synopsis from Ross, I immersed myself in researching and exploring visual themes, drawing inspiration from both classic and contemporary sci-fi designs.

I presented several options, and together we decided on the final approach. From there, I completed the cover design and worked on the rest of the book's layout.
